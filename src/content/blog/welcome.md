This is the first post on **The Undercurrent Show** — a home on the web for
writing that lives outside the feed.

## Why a blog, and why now

Publishing on someone else's platform means renting your audience. Here, the
domain is mine, the words are mine, and nothing sits between a reader and the
page. No paywall, no popups, no algorithm deciding who sees what.

## What to expect

- **Essays** on the ideas moving quietly under the surface.
- **Notes** — shorter, rougher, more frequent.
- The occasional deep dive when a topic earns it.

## How to follow along

Grab the [RSS feed](/rss.xml) and you'll get every new post in your reader of
choice. That's it — no signup required.
